Using GPS

Tapping "Start Point" will place a pin on the map of your current location. This will also start the automatic timer. Every 5 minutes your phone will drip a GPS pin on your map. By choosing "Current Map" you can see your current map with all the pins placed on it.

If you'd like to set a manual pin, press "Set Manual Pin." You'll be prompted to enter a note so you can remember what you pinned. These are great for points of interest or landmarks.

To end your GPS tracking, simply press "End GPS Tracking." Your map and pins will be saved in your phone until you choose to delete the map.

Your Data

The goal with Trail Companion is to make sure you are never alone while in the back country. We use your GPS on your phone to store your location every 5 minutes even if you don't have cell service.

But, we never store your information. Security is our number one priority. All the GPS data is saved locally on the phone and you have complete control over your data. We don't store any GPS data in our servers.
