Start GPS Tracking

You will be asked to name your trail. You can always go back and change the name later. Every 5 minutes your phone will drop a GPS pin on your map.

Manual Pin Set

If you'd like to set a manual pin, press "Set Manual Pin." You'll be prompted to enter a note so you can remember what you pinned. These are great for points of interest or landmarks.

End GPS Tracking

Simply press "End GPS Tracking." Your map and pins will be saved in your phone until you choose to delete the map.
